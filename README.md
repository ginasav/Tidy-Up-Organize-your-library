# 📚 Tidy Up: Organize your library

> *Finally, a library your mom will approve of!*

**Tidy Up: Organize your library** is a smart mobile app that solves the eternal struggle of organizing your personal library. No more books falling off shelves, no more wasted space, no more frustrated rearranging. Just perfectly organized bookshelves, every time.

## 🎯 The Problem

We've all been there:
- Books scattered everywhere because they don't fit properly
- Thick books taking up too much space while thin ones get lost
- Constantly rearranging shelves trying to make everything fit
- Wasted shelf space that could hold more books
- The eternal mom complaint: *"Your room is a disaster!"*

## ✨ The Solution

Tidy Up uses your phone's camera and advanced measurement algorithms to:

1. **Measure Your Books** - Add your book details: title, author, pages
2. **Analyze Your Shelves** - Measure shelf dimensions and spacing
3. **Optimize Organization** - Calculate the perfect arrangement for maximum efficiency

## 🚀 Key Features

### 📏 Smart Measurement
- **Shelf measurement tools** using AR technology
- **Automatic thickness detection** for optimal spacing

### 📱 User-Friendly Interface
- **Drag-and-drop** book planning
- **Colorful** shelves and books to see them distinctly


## 📱 User Experience

### Onboarding Flow
1. **Welcome & Tutorial** - Quick intro to app capabilities
2. **Shelf Creation** - Create your first shelf
3. **Collection Creation** - Add the books you want in that shelf
4. **Shelf Scanning** - Measure your available shelf space
5. **Organization Magic** - Get your optimized layout plan
